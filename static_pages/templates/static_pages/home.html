What is UPTM Housing Hub?

The UPTM Housing Registration System seeks to establish a centralized web platform to replace the present reliance on MPP Instagram stories and Telegram groups for housing and roommate searches. This platform will be aimed to improve organization and accessibility for UPTM students by offering a simple interface for publishing and locating housing-related information. The system will prioritize transparency in housing listings by providing comprehensive and accurate information about available rooms and rental agreements. The product will act as a comprehensive resource for students, increasing the overall efficiency of the housing hunt.

Objectives of this websites is to:

  • To develop centralized platforms: The primary goal is to replace current reliance on MPP's Instagram stories and Telegram groups with a centralized web platform for housing ads and roommate searching. This established approach is intended to improve organization and accessibility for UPTM students.
  • To integrate real-time updates: This goal entails providing features that enable real-time updates within the centralized platform. These features are designed to ensure that students have immediate access to the latest information by enabling them to view new posts seamlessly.
  • promote transparency in listings: Implement aspects that enhance transparency in housing advertisements, such as extensive and accurate information about available rooms, and rental agreements. This transparency enables students to make more informed decisions
